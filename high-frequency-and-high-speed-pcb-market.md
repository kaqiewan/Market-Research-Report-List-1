<p><p>The High Frequency and High Speed PCB market consists primarily of two types: double-sided boards and multilayer boards. Double-sided boards feature conductive pathways on both sides, ideal for moderate complexity and cost-effectiveness. Multilayer boards, comprising three or more layers, offer enhanced performance and higher routing density, making them suitable for advanced applications like RF communication and high-speed data transfer. Both types cater to industries requiring efficient signal integrity and minimized electromagnetic interference in compact designs.</p></p>
