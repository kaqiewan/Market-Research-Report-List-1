<p><ul><li>Color Resists(RGB)</li><li>Black Matrix(BM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD photoresist market is primarily categorized into Color Resists (RGB) and Black Matrix (BM) types. Color Resists are essential for creating vivid display images, as they enable the formation of red, green, and blue pixels, crucial for color accuracy. Black Matrix, on the other hand, enhances display contrast by absorbing light and minimizing color overlap between pixels, improving overall image quality. Both types are vital for the production of high-resolution LCD screens with vibrant visuals and deeper contrasts.</p></p>
