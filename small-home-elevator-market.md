<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Traction</li><li>Machine-Room-Less</li><li>Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small home elevator market includes several types: Hydraulic elevators, which use fluid pressure for movement and require a machine room; Traction elevators, which rely on cables and pulleys for vertical transport, offering smoother rides; Machine-Room-Less (MRL) elevators, designed to save space with components housed within the shaft; and Vacuum elevators, which utilize air pressure to move cab upward, providing a unique, energy-efficient option. Each type caters to different homeowner needs, balancing efficiency, space, and cost.</p></p>
