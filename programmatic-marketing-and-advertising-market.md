<p><p>Programmatic marketing and advertising use automated technology to buy and sell ad inventory in real time. There are two main market types: Real-Time Bidding (RTB) and programmatic direct. RTB is an open marketplace where advertisers bid on ad space in milliseconds, optimizing costs and reach. In contrast, programmatic direct involves pre-negotiated deals between publishers and advertisers, ensuring specific inventory at agreed prices while providing greater control and predictability over campaigns. Both enhance targeting and efficiency in advertising.</p></p>
