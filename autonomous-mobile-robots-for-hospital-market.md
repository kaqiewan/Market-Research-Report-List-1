<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for hospitals can be categorized into two main types: LiDAR-based and LiDAR+Vision-based systems. LiDAR-based AMRs utilize laser scanning technology for navigation and obstacle detection, offering precise localization and mapping in dynamic environments. In contrast, LiDAR+Vision-based AMRs enhance their capabilities by integrating visual sensors, enabling better understanding of complex surroundings, such as recognizing people and objects. Both systems aim to improve efficiency in hospital operations, like delivery and transportation tasks.</p></p>
