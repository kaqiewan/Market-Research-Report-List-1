<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Air Conditioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle air conditioner market predominantly features two types: reciprocating and rotary. Reciprocating air conditioners use pistons to compress refrigerant, offering effective cooling but with more moving parts, resulting in increased noise and wear. In contrast, rotary air conditioners utilize a rotating mechanism for compression, providing smoother operation, reduced noise, and a more compact design. Both types are pivotal in ensuring passenger comfort but cater to different priorities in performance, efficiency, and installation space within vehicles.</p></p>
