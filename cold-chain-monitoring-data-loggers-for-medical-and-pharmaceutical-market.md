<p><p>Cold chain monitoring data loggers are essential for maintaining the integrity of temperature-sensitive medical and pharmaceutical products. Offline data loggers store temperature data locally and require manual retrieval for analysis. Cellular connected data loggers transmit real-time data through cellular networks, allowing for continuous monitoring and immediate alerts. Wireless connected data loggers use Wi-Fi or other wireless technologies to transmit data, enabling remote monitoring and easier integration with digital platforms. Each type serves to ensure compliance and product safety throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
