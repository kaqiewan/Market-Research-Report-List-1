<p><ul><li>Alcohol-base Type</li><li>Non-alcohol Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The instrument disinfectants market is divided into two primary types: alcohol-based and non-alcohol-based disinfectants. Alcohol-based disinfectants typically contain ethanol or isopropanol, providing rapid killing action against a broad spectrum of pathogens. They are widely used due to their effectiveness and evaporating properties. Non-alcohol-based disinfectants, on the other hand, may include quaternary ammonium compounds, chlorine-based agents, or other formulations, often featuring longer contact times and residual activity. Both types are essential for ensuring safe and sterile medical environments.</p></p>
