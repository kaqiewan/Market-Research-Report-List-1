Pure gases consist of single gas elements or compounds, such as oxygen, nitrogen, and helium, utilized in various industrial, medical, and scientific applications. Gas mixtures, on the other hand, are combinations of multiple gases tailored for specific functions, like calibration gases for analytical equipment or process gases for electronics manufacturing. Both types are essential for diverse applications in industries ranging from healthcare to environmental monitoring.</p></p>
