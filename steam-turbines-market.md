<p><p>The steam turbines market is primarily segmented into steam cycle and combined cycle types. The steam cycle market features turbines that generate electricity using steam produced from heated water, primarily in conventional power plants. In contrast, the combined cycle market integrates both gas and steam turbines for enhanced efficiency; exhaust gases from the gas turbine heat water to produce steam, maximizing energy output. This dual-functionality makes combined cycle systems more efficient and increasingly popular in modern energy production.</p></p>
