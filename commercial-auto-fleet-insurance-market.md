<p><p>The commercial auto fleet insurance market encompasses two primary types: compulsory and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is mandated by law, ensuring that all vehicles in a fleet have a minimum level of coverage to protect against liability and injuries in case of an accident. Conversely, optional insurance allows businesses to tailor their coverage based on specific needs, including comprehensive and collision coverage, which offer broader protection for vehicles and assets, safeguarding against theft, damage, and other risks.</p></p>
