<p><p>A Reciprocating Gait Orthosis (RGO) is a specialized device designed to assist individuals with lower limb disabilities in achieving a more natural walking pattern. It is typically used by individuals with conditions such as spinal cord injuries or neuromuscular disorders, facilitating mobility by linking movements of the legs through a system of cables and harnesses. This mechanism allows the user to walk in a reciprocal manner by shifting weight from one leg to the other, thus promoting better locomotor function and independence.</p><p>The Reciprocating Gait Orthosis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period. Trends such as the development of customized and lightweight orthotic solutions, along with the integration of smart technologies for real-time adjustments, are shaping the future of the market. <p><p>The Reciprocating Gait Orthosis (RGO) market is segmented into three main types: Horizontal Cable RGO, Rocker Bar RGO, and Isocentric RGO. Horizontal Cable RGOs utilize a cable system to facilitate movement between legs, promoting a natural gait. Rocker Bar RGOs incorporate a curved bar to assist with weight transfer and balance. Isocentric RGOs feature a rotational mechanism that allows simultaneous leg movement, enhancing gait efficiency. Each type addresses specific mobility needs and supports individuals with gait impairments.</p></p>
