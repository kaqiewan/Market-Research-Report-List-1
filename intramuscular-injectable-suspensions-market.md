<p><p>The intramuscular injectable suspensions market can be categorized into single-dose and multiple-dose injections. Single-dose injections contain a pre-measured quantity of medication for one-time use, ensuring precise dosing and reducing contamination risk. Conversely, multiple-dose injections allow for several doses to be drawn from a single vial over time, providing convenience and cost-effectiveness but increasing the potential for contamination and dosing errors. Both types cater to different therapeutic needs and patient preferences in drug administration.</p></p>
