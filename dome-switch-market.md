<p><ul><li>SMD Mount</li><li>Through Hole Mounting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dome switch market is categorized into three main types: SMD Mount, Through Hole Mounting, and Others. SMD (Surface-Mounted Device) Mount switches are designed for efficient surface mounting on circuit boards, offering compactness and ease of assembly. Through Hole Mounting involves inserting the switch leads through holes in the PCB, providing robust connectivity, especially in high-stress applications. The "Others" category includes specialized or less common mounting options, catering to niche applications. Each type serves distinct functional and design requirements in electronic devices.</p></p>
