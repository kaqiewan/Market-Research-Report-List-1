<p><strong>The Thermal Management EV Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct</li><li>Indirect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal management EV fluid market comprises two primary types: direct and indirect cooling systems. Direct cooling systems circulate fluids directly around battery cells, ensuring effective heat dissipation and maintaining optimal operating temperatures. In contrast, indirect cooling systems utilize a heat exchanger to remove heat from the battery, allowing a separate fluid loop to manage temperature regulation. Both systems are essential for enhancing battery performance, extending lifespan, and ensuring safety in electric vehicles, catering to different design and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
