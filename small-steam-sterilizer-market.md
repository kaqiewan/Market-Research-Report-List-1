<p><strong>The Small Steam Sterilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>SFPP</li><li>Pre-Vac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small steam sterilizer market is segmented into three main types: Gravity, Steam Flush Pressure Pulse (SFPP), and Pre-Vacuum sterilizers. Gravity sterilizers rely on gravity to drive steam into the chamber, suitable for simple sterilization needs. SFPP combines steam flush and pressure pulses to enhance air removal, ensuring effective sterilization in challenging loads. Pre-Vac sterilizers use a vacuum to remove air before steam infiltration, optimizing the sterilization process for porous and complex instruments, providing superior results in healthcare settings.</p></p>
