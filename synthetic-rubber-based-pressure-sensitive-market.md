<p><p>The synthetic rubber-based pressure-sensitive market encompasses two primary types: solvent-based and hot-melt types. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents to dissolve synthetic rubber, providing strong adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for various applications like labels and tapes. In contrast, hot-melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that bond upon cooling after being heated. They offer quick bonding and ease of application, often used in packaging and assembly operations. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and performance criteria.</p></p>
