<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Suture Thread Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-term Absorbable</li><li>Mid-term Absorbable</li><li>Long-term Absorbable</li><li>Non-Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suture thread market encompasses various types based on absorption duration. Short-term absorbable sutures dissolve within days to weeks, suitable for superficial wounds. Mid-term absorbable sutures last several weeks to months, ideal for deeper tissue healing. Long-term absorbable sutures remain effective for an extended period, providing prolonged support for complex procedures. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, do not degrade and are used for permanent closure needs, making them essential in specific surgical applications where lasting support is necessary.</p></p>
