Enhanced technological innovations, such as miniaturized devices with improved battery life and wireless connectivity, are also contributing to market growth. Additionally, the growing aging population and the need for long-term monitoring of heart health are further propelling demand for ILMs.</p><p>Emerging trends include the integration of artificial intelligence for better data interpretation, increasing awareness about heart health, and improvements in healthcare infrastructure, particularly in emerging economies. Companies are focusing on developing user-friendly models that allow for remote monitoring, thereby expanding their patient reach. The convergence of telemedicine and implantable devices is expected to transform patient care, making ILMs a critical component of modern cardiology practices.</p></p>
