<p><p>Centrifugal humidifiers are categorized into three primary market types: ceiling-mounted units, portable units, and standing floor units. Ceiling-mounted units are designed for large spaces, using high airflow to distribute humidity effectively throughout the area. Portable units offer flexibility, easily moving between rooms to provide localized humidity control. Standing floor units combine mobility with a larger capacity, often used in medium to large spaces, ensuring optimal humidity levels while blending into various environments. Each type serves specific needs for effective humidity management.</p></p>
