<p><p>The module grade current sensor market encompasses three main types: Hall-based current sensors, shunt-based current sensors, and others. Hall-based sensors utilize the Hall effect to measure current without direct electrical contact, offering isolation and high accuracy. Shunt-based sensors, on the other hand, rely on a low-resistance resistor to detect current flow, providing a straightforward and cost-effective solution for low-voltage applications.
