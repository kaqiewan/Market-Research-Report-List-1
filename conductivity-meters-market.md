<p><p>Conductivity meters are categorized into three primary market types: portable, in-line, and bench-top. Portable meters are compact devices ideal for field measurements, providing convenience and ease of use for quick assessments. In-line meters are integrated into processes or systems, continuously monitoring conductivity for real-time data in industrial applications. Bench-top meters offer high precision and advanced features, suitable for laboratory settings, enabling detailed analysis and extensive testing. Each type serves distinct needs in various industries, including environmental, food, and chemical sectors.</p></p>
