<p><p>The polyester adhesives market comprises several key components: solvents, hardening initiators, hardening catalysts, and extenders. Solvents help dissolve the polyester resin, enhancing flow and application. Hardening initiators trigger the curing process, while hardening catalysts accelerate the reaction, ensuring rapid bonding. Extenders are added to improve product properties, such as flexibility and strength, and to reduce costs. Together, these elements create versatile adhesives suitable for various applications in industries like automotive, construction, and packaging.</p></p>
