<p><p>The non-embedded pico projector market includes three primary types: Digital Light Processing (DLP), which uses micro-mirrors to create images with high brightness and color accuracy; Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS), which employs liquid crystal technology for sharp image quality and is often used in high-end models; and Laser Beam Steering (LBS), which utilizes laser diodes for precision light projection, allowing for compact designs and enhanced portability. Each technology serves specific needs in terms of performance and application.</p></p>
