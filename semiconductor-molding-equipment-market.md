<p><p>The semiconductor molding equipment market consists of three main types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual systems. Fully automatic equipment offers high efficiency and minimal operator intervention, making it suitable for large-scale production. Semi-automatic systems strike a balance, requiring some manual input while still enhancing productivity and consistency. Manual molding equipment relies heavily on human operation, providing flexibility and lower initial costs, but is typically slower and more prone to variability in quality. Each type serves different production needs and scales.</p></p>
