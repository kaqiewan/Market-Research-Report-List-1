<p><p>The advanced targeted drug delivery market encompasses various delivery systems, including liposomes, microspheres, nanoparticles, and emulsions. Liposomes are spherical vesicles that encapsulate drugs, enhancing their bioavailability. Microspheres are solid or hollow particles that improve drug stability and release rates. Nanoparticles offer a high surface area for drug attachment, enabling precise targeting and controlled release. Emulsions, mixing oil and water, facilitate the delivery of lipophilic drugs while improving their absorption. Together, these systems enhance therapeutic effectiveness and reduce side effects.</p></p>
