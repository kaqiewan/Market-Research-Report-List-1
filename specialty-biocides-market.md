<p><p>The Specialty Biocides Market is categorized into inorganic and organic biocides. Inorganic biocides include compounds like metals and salts, effective in killing microorganisms through mechanisms that disrupt cellular processes. They are commonly used in water treatment and industrial applications. Organic biocides, on the other hand, consist of carbon-based compounds, including phenols and amines, targeting specific biological pathways. These are prevalent in fields such as agriculture and personal care, providing targeted efficacy against specific pests and pathogens.</p></p>
