<p><p>Automatic marking systems are crucial for various industries, utilizing different technologies like laser and inkjet for effective product labeling. Laser marking systems employ high-intensity lasers to etch permanent markings on surfaces, ideal for durable applications and materials like metal and plastic. In contrast, inkjet marking systems use liquid ink to create variable messages, barcodes, or graphics, suitable for high-speed production lines and varying substrate types. Both technologies cater to distinct marking requirements, enhancing efficiency and traceability in manufacturing.</p></p>
