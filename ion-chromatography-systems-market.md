<p><p>The ion chromatography systems market encompasses various types, including ion exchange chromatography, ion exclusion chromatography, and ion pair chromatography. Ion exchange chromatography separates ions based on their charge, effectively quantifying cations and anions. Ion exclusion chromatography focuses on separating charged species while excluding neutral ions, making it suitable for organic compounds. Ion pair chromatography combines charged molecules with neutral ion pairs to enhance separation efficiency, particularly for complex samples. Each method caters to specific analytical needs across industries.</p></p>
