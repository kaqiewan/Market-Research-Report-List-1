<p><p>The die bonding paste adhesive market is segmented into conductive and non-conductive types. Conductive adhesives facilitate electrical connections between components, making them essential in electronics for high-performance applications where reliable conductivity is critical. Non-conductive adhesives, on the other hand, provide mechanical bonding without electrical pathways, suitable for insulating applications or areas where electrical interaction is unnecessary. Both types serve vital roles in semiconductor packaging and electronic assembly, addressing specific performance requirements based on application needs.</p></p>
