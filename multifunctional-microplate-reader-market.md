<p><p>The multifunctional microplate reader market features two primary types: filter type and raster microplate readers. Filter type microplate readers utilize specific optical filters to measure absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence, allowing for versatile assays in various applications. In contrast, raster microplate readers employ a scanning mechanism to detect signals across multiple wells simultaneously, enhancing speed and efficiency for high-throughput screening. Both types serve critical roles in research and clinical laboratories, providing essential data for a wide range of biochemical analyses.</p></p>
