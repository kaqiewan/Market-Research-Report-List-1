<p><p>Mechanically Fastened Cross-laminated Timber (MFCLT) is an engineered wood product made by layering sheets of timber in different orientations, with mechanical fasteners securing the layers together. This design enhances structural integrity and stability, making MFCLT an attractive alternative to traditional construction materials like steel and concrete. It is particularly prized for its sustainability, as it utilizes renewable resources and reduces carbon footprints, appealing to both environmentally conscious builders and consumers.</p><p>The MFCLT market is poised for significant growth, driven by increasing demand for sustainable construction solutions, a rise in urbanization, and a focus on reducing construction waste.
