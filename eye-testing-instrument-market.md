<p><p>The eye testing instrument market includes various devices essential for comprehensive eye examinations. Slit lamps provide detailed imaging of the anterior eye structures. Biometers measure eye length and curvature for accurate lens prescription. Perimeters assess visual fields, aiding in glaucoma detection. Tonometers measure intraocular pressure to screen for glaucoma. Fundus cameras capture images of the retina and optic nerve. Autorefractors determine refractive errors, while keratometers measure corneal curvature. Other instruments complement these functions, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in ophthalmology.</p></p>
