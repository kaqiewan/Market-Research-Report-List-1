<p><p>The Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) market is primarily divided into two types: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to clamp voltage spikes in one polarity, making them ideal for single-polarity applications. Conversely, Bi-polar TVS devices can handle voltage transients in both polarities, providing a broader range of protection for circuits that may experience bidirectional transients. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing market demand based on device specifications and requirements.</p></p>
