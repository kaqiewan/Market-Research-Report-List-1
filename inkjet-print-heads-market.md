<p><strong>The Inkjet Print Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type (Bulk Piezo, Thin Film Piezo)</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet print heads market includes two main types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric print heads utilize piezoelectric materials to create pressure, enabling precise ink delivery; they are further categorized into bulk piezo and thin film piezo types. Bulk piezo offers durability and stability, while thin film piezo provides higher resolution and smaller form factors. Thermal print heads, on the other hand, use heat to vaporize ink, creating bubbles that push ink onto the substrate, commonly found in consumer printers for affordability and simplicity.</p></p>
