<p><p>The Network Surveillance Cameras market consists of two main types: centralized IP cameras and decentralized IP cameras. Centralized IP cameras are connected to a central recording server, enabling efficient management and storage of video data, making it suitable for larger installations. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras operate independently, storing footage locally or in the cloud, offering flexibility and easier scalability for varying network infrastructures. Both types cater to different security needs and deployment scenarios, enhancing surveillance capabilities across various environments.</p></p>
