Electric pruners offer convenience and ease of use, powered by batteries or cords, suitable for home gardeners seeking efficiency. Manual pruners, the most traditional type, rely on human strength and dexterity, favored for their simplicity and precision in smaller garden spaces. Each type caters to varying user needs and preferences.</p></p>
