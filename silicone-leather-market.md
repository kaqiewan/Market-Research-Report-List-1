<p><p>The silicone leather market encompasses two main types: silicone resin leather and silicone rubber leather. Silicone resin leather features a durable, resin-based surface that mimics traditional leather while offering enhanced resistance to water, UV rays, and abrasion. It is often used in high-end applications like automotive interiors and furniture. On the other hand, silicone rubber leather provides a flexible, soft texture ideal for fashion items and accessories, known for its excellent elasticity and variety in colors. Both types are eco-friendly alternatives to conventional leather.</p></p>
