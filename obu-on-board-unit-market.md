<p><strong>The OBU (On Board Unit) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sngle-chip OBU (Without IC Card Interface)</li><li>Double-chip OBU (With IC Card Interface)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On Board Unit (OBU) market encompasses two primary types: single-chip OBUs and double-chip OBUs. Single-chip OBUs do not feature an Integrated Circuit (IC) card interface, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, double-chip OBUs include an IC card interface, enabling advanced functionalities like secure transactions and enhanced data management. This differentiation caters to varying needs in transportation systems, such as toll collection or access controls, highlighting their roles in efficient operational frameworks.</p></p>
