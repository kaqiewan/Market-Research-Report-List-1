<p><p>Anti-seismic support systems are essential for mitigating the effects of earthquakes on structures. The cylindrical support type provides stability through its rounded shape, allowing for flexibility and movement. Bushing supports utilize rubber or other compliant materials to absorb shock and reduce vibrations, enhancing overall safety. Conical supports feature a tapered design that aids in load distribution and stability during seismic events. Each type serves a unique function to protect buildings and infrastructure from seismic forces, contributing to resilience in earthquake-prone areas.</p></p>
