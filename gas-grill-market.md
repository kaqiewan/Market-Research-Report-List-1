<p><p>The gas grill market primarily consists of two main types: propane gas grills and natural gas grills. Propane gas grills are portable and fueled by propane tanks, making them ideal for camping and outdoor events. In contrast, natural gas grills are connected to a home’s gas line, providing a constant fuel supply and eliminating the need for tank replacements. Both options offer convenience and efficiency, catering to different user preferences and outdoor cooking needs, enhancing the overall grilling experience.</p></p>
