<p><p>The medical grade gloves market includes various types such as latex, nitrile, PVC, and others. Latex gloves offer excellent elasticity and comfort, making them popular in healthcare settings but may cause allergic reactions. Nitrile gloves are a durable alternative, resistant to punctures and chemicals, ideal for those with latex allergies. PVC gloves are economical and suitable for low-risk tasks, though they lack the flexibility of latex and nitrile. Other materials may include neoprene or vinyl, tailored for specific uses in the medical field.</p></p>
