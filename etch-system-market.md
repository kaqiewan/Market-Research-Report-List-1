<p><p>The Etch System Market encompasses various technologies for material removal in semiconductor fabrication. Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) is known for deep etching with high precision. Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) combines physical and chemical processes for anisotropic etching, ideal for complex geometries. Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) specializes in creating deep, high aspect-ratio structures, essential for MEMS applications. Other techniques include plasma etching and laser etching, each serving specific applications across industries like electronics, telecommunications, and microfabrication.</p></p>
